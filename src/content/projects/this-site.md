This is the site you're looking at. It's a small, fast, statically generated
personal site built with **Astro 6** and **Tailwind CSS 4**.

## Notes

- Fully internationalized with URL-prefixed locales (`/`, `/pt`, `/es`).
- Content-driven: bio, CV, projects and posts all live in Markdown.
- Light and dark themes, an RSS feed and a tag-based content index.
- Deployed to Cloudflare Pages.

It's a sibling to my engineering-focused site, [djalmajr.dev](https://djalmajr.dev).
